LEADING SPONSORS

Colgate is an umbrella brand principally used for oral hygiene products such as toothpastes, toothbrushes, mouthwashes and dental floss. Manufactured by Colgate-Palmolive, Colgate oral hygiene products were first sold by the company in 1873, sixteen years after the death of the founder, William Colgate.





The Times of India is an Indian English-language daily newspaper owned by The Times Group. It is the third-largest newspaper in India by circulation and largest selling English-language daily in the world. according to Audit Bureau of Circulations.





The State Bank of India is an Indian multinational, public sector banking and financial services statutory body. It is a government corporation statutory body headquartered in Mumbai, Maharashtra. SBI is ranked as 236th in the Fortune Global 500 list of the world's biggest corporations of 2019.





Dettol is a British brand of cleaning supplies and disinfectant owned by Reckitt Benckiser. They sell a variety of different forms of household sanitary cleaners, ranging from thin mist spray disinfectant to wipes. They come with different scents. The original Dettol Antiseptic Disinfectant Liquid contains the antiseptic chloroxylenol.





Atria Convergence Technologies Limited., branded as ACT, is an Indian telecommunications company headquartered in Bangalore, Karnataka. ACT offers fiber to the home services under the brand name "ACT Fibernet" and digital television services under the "ACT Digital" brand.
